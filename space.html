Space Filling Diagram


Interactivity

Using tooltip, you can see the name of the case and the amount of cases related to that. Mouse over to green circles to see the type of any case. Also by clicking on neighborhood cells you can zoom on that specific neighborhood.

Encoding

Biggest lap(root in yellow) is showing the city which is San Francisco.
Green laps show the call type groups.
Dark Blue laps show the call type and how many of these cases happened per call type group in the city.
Purple laps or the smallests show final disposition.(Purple)

Wrangling

Data has been filtered using DataSF website to show only cases related to March 2020, city of San Francisco.

Conclusion

In Conclusion, plot gives us the overall overview of the calls in respect to neighborhoods. We can understand that 2 neighborhoods (Tenderloin and South Market) with 2k and 1.6k cases had the most cases. .
